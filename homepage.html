Welcome to the KenGen ICT Equipment Issuance Log.

This system is designed to efficiently track the issuance and allocation of ICT equipment within KenGen.
By logging each issuance, we ensure accurate record-keeping, streamlined asset management, and accountability for all ICT resources.
This tool is integral to maintaining operational efficiency and supporting KenGen's commitment to leveraging technology for improved service delivery.
Please use the navigation links to log new issuances, view existing records, and generate reports as needed.
